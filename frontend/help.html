Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a job application? ▼
Go to the Dashboard page and click the "Add Application" button. Fill in the form and click Submit.
Can I edit or delete an application later? ▼
Yes, each card has Edit and Delete buttons to modify or remove entries.
How do I upload supporting documents like resumes? ▼
Navigate to the Documents page, select a file, and click Upload. You can also delete files later.
What happens when I log out? ▼
Logging out clears your session and redirects you to the login page. You can log back in anytime.
I forgot my password. How do I reset it? ▼
Go to the Settings page and use the “Change Password” section to set a new password.
